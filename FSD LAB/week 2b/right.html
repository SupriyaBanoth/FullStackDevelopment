The creator of "Pinch of Yum" turned a hobby into a thriving business that now reaches millions with its delicious recipes. They reside in Saint Paul, MN, with their husband, Bjork, and their dog Sage, cherishing pad Thai, sunny days, and visits to the dog park.

They share fresh, flavorful, and mostly healthy recipes inspired by their everyday life, aiming to make cooking both approachable and exciting. Their goal is to inspire others to eagerly look forward to cooking for themselves, family, or friends.

Cooking is where creativity meets comfort, turning simple ingredients into unforgettable moments.
